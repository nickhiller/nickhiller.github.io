Schneider

"The original Bavarian wheat beer, brewed by authority of the king. In 1872, due to the rise of the Czech Pilsner style, the Bavarian wheat beer (hefeweizen) was facing extinction. Enter Georg Schneider I who purchased the exclusive royal brewing rights to hefeweizen from Ludwig II, King of Bavaria, and set up shop in downtown Munich. 140 years and six generations of Georg Schneiders later, we’re sure glad he did!"

— Product description that I wrote for Schneider's U.S. marketing. There is no better hefeweizen, IMHO.
